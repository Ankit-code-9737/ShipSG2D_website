Our Vision

We want to bring inspiration and innovation to every person by connecting makers with their communities, beyond borders.

Every day, Easyship’s independent thinkers, technologists, and operators work collaboratively on compelling projects, inspiring each other to bring this vision to fruition.

Join a fast-growing company that is building sophisticated tools to help SMBs scale and share products in a more innovative way.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make shipping frictionless from checkout to delivery by providing accessible, best-in-class shipping solutions to all merchants, effectively removing barriers like inefficient systems and prohibitive costs for SMBs.

With headquarters in New York and Hong Kong, and offices in London, Singapore, Taipei, and Melbourne, our global team works tirelessly to demystify the complex world of logistics for over 40,000 clients worldwide.
